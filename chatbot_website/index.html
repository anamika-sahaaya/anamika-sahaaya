Incidents

A graphic video of two women being sexually assaulted in public in the northeastern state of Manipur has sparked outrage and condemnation. Police have arrested at least four people on suspicion of kidnapping and gang rape and are searching for at least 30 others.

In Kerala, over 1,000 children were sexually abused in their own homes in 2022, according to a report by the Kerala State Commission for Protection of Child Rights. Among the 4,582 child abuse cases registered in Kerala in 2022, in as many as 1,004 cases, the places of occurrence of the crime were the houses of the survivors or victims.

In July 2022, a transgender woman was raped in New Delhi. The case was widely covered in the media, but it highlights the ongoing challenges faced by transgenders in India, including their vulnerability to sexual violence and the need for more inclusive rape laws

Sexual violence against men in India is a significant issue, although it often goes unreported and unaddressed.A study conducted in the state of Tamil Nadu, India, found that 56% of the men surveyed had experienced some form of sexual violence in their lifetime, with 26% experiencing it in the last year
